2.  Describe the purpose of a DIV tag.

A div tag allows you to assign classes or IDs to sections of code. Those classes or IDs can then be styled with in-line styling or in a linked CSS stylesheet. IDs are intended for single use and override other style values, whereas classes can be used over and over.

12. What is the main difference between a serif and a sans-serif font?

Serif has little "swoops" at the end of letters that are meant to guide the eye from one letter to another, whereas sans-serif does not have these, instead ending letters with clean lines. Sans-serif is often used on the web (sleeker, easier to read) while Serif is often used in print.
