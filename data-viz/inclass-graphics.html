What race are school shooters likely to be?

White individuals are the most likely demographic to commit school shootings, accouting for 60 of 105 shootings since Columbine in 1999.

Source: Data collected by The Washington Post



Where do school shootings occur?

Almost half of school shootings take place in cities, with 101 shooting incidents out of a total of 214. The majority of Americans do, however, live in cities: cities are home to 62.7 percent of the population according to a 2015 U.S. Census Bureau report.

Source: Data collected by The Washington Post



Examining the carnage

The number of deaths from school shootings has generally remained below 10, with notable exceptions in 2012 with 32 deaths and 2018, with 21 deaths already. Since Columbine, a total of 131 individuals have died in school schootings and a further 402 injured.

Source: Data collected by The Washington Post
